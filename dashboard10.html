Score Methodology. The Investment Opportunity Score (IOS) is a proprietary composite index calculated from six equally weighted components: (1) 12-month price growth rate, (2) 12-month rent growth rate, (3) Ironwood Liquidity Score (0–10), (4) inverse of pipeline risk score (lower risk = higher score), (5) gross yield estimate, and (6) population growth proxy. Each component is normalised to a 0–10 scale. The final IOS is the unweighted average. This index is for analytical reference only and does not constitute investment advice.
