Our Mission

Ironwood Intelligence exists to bring transparency, data, and institutional-grade analytics to real estate markets across Africa. We aggregate, clean, and structure property data from 35+ countries so investors, developers, and institutions can make smarter decisions.

Based in Meydan Free Zone, Dubai, UAE — operating across the African continent, tracking thousands of properties in real time.
